For almost ten years, Amsterdam based photographer Thomas Schlijper has been exhibiting fresh work every day, 365 days a year, on his website schlijper.nl. Now it is possible to view his daily photos on your iPhone. The second version of the schlijper.nl app not only allows you to browse through his latest photos, but also to view photos in thumbnail mode, enlarge photos by pinching and swipe to move through photos. An e-mail function is also added.

Enjoy and feel free to post a comment!
